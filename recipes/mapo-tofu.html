Mapo Tofu Recipe

Spicy Mapo Tofu

If you like some 'spice' in your life, check this recipe out!

Ingredients

  • 2 blocks of firm tofu (~ 12 oz. per block)
  • 2 onions (diced)
  • 1 cup of mushrooms (sliced)
  • 4 cloves of garlic (minced.. add to your liking)
  • 2-3 stalks of green onions (chopped)
  • 3 tablespoons of Doubanjiang (spicy fermented bean paste)
  • 2 tablespoons of mirin (cooking wine)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of powdered ginger
  • 2 teaspoons of chili oil
  • 3 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large saut√© pan on medium heat and cook diced onions until they start to brown.
  2. Add mushrooms and spicy fermented bean paste (Doubanjiang) and mix until evenly combined.
  3. Stir in chili oil, ginger, soy sauce, garlic, 5-spice powder, mirin, and sugar.
  4. Cut the tofu into bite-sized pieces and arrange them on top of the other ingredients in the pan. Pour 1 1/2 cups of hot water over the tofu and gently mix.
  5. Cover the pan and simmer over medium-low heat for about 20 minutes or until the tofu is tender.
  6. Serve over jasmine rice, garnished with green onions.
