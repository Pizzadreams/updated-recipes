Brownies!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound unsalted butter
  • 1 pound plus 12 ounces semisweet chocolate chip
  • 6 ounces bitter chocolate
  • 6 extra-large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons instant coffee granules
  • 2 tablespoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2-1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Grease and flour a 12 x 18 x 1-inch baking pan.
  3. In a medium bowl, melt the butter, 1 pound of chocolate chips, and bitter chocolate over simmering water. Let it cool slightly.
  4. In a separate large bowl, mix the eggs, coffee granules, vanilla, and sugar gently (not too much).
  5. Incorporate the cooled chocolate mixture into the egg mixture and let it cool to room temperature.
  6. In another medium bowl, sift 1 cup of flour, baking powder, and salt together. Add it to the cooled chocolate mixture.
  7. In a separate medium bowl, coat the walnuts and 12 ounces of chocolate chips with 1/4 cup of flour. Then add it to the chocolate batter.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  9. Bake for 20 minutes, then tap the pan against the oven shelf to release any trapped air between the pan and the batter. Bake for an additional 15 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Don't overbake!
  10. Allow the brownies to cool completely, then refrigerate and cut into 20 large squares.
