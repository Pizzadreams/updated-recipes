Fettuccini Alfredo

The classic fettuccini alfredo

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine noodles
  • 1/2 Cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups of Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 tbsp of fresh Italian parsley, chopped
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Fill pot with cold water and add salt.
  3. Add fettuccine into the water after it's boiled. Cook 'til al dente.
  4. In another pan, heat butter and cream over medium-low heat.
  5. Lower heat and simmer over 10-15 minutes. Remove from heat.
  6. Add cheese and stir until fully mixed. Adjust consistency with cream if necessary.
  7. Stir in garnish. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  8. Drain noodles and pour in Alfredo sauce.
  9. Add parmesan cheese and other garnish.
